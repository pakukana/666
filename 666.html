BANGKOK

There're a lot of things to do in Bangkok. Bangkok, Thailand's capital, is a large exciting city. Bangkok has been my 'home' since I visited for the first time. Bangkok, in the most populous city, has many good restaurants, shopping malls, and nightlife activity.

MARKET

Discover the best night market in Bangkok. Night markets are the best way to enjoy Thai local food, shopping, and drinking. There are many nihgt markets in Bangkok.It's a must-do night activity. Here are some of the major markets, Ratchada Night Market, Silom-Patpong Night market, On nut Night Market, Talad Rot Fai Srinakarin Market.

TEMPLES

Wat Phra Kaew (Temple of Emerald Buddha) and Wat Pho are must-go temples in Bangkok! Buddhism is an important part of Thailand culture. Enjoy and experience the greatest historical places in Bangkok.

OTHER

Phuket island is one of the famouse place for tourists. There are Phuket towns, the beaches, old buildings, and lots more!

Phuket City, on Phuket Island, is the capital of Thailand’s Phuket Province. In the Old Town, Thalang Road is lined with colorful 19th-century shophouses and Sino-Portuguese buildings. Built in 1903 by a wealthy tin merchant, Baan Chinpracha mansion has Italian floor tiles, shuttered windows and antique furniture. Set in a 1930s manor, the Thai Hua Museum has exhibits on Phuket’s culture and history.(Wikipedia)
